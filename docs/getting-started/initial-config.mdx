Now that your device has firmware installed, you need to connect it to a client app and set your region before you can communicate over the mesh.

## Step 1: Choose a Client App

Meshtastic offers several client apps. Which ones you can use depends on how you connect to your device:

**Bluetooth** is the most common way to connect. Available on most devices.

- [Android App](/docs/category/android-app) — Full-featured, recommended for most users

**USB Serial** connects your device directly to a computer via cable.

- [Web Client](https://client.meshtastic.org) — Works in Chrome/Edge browsers
- [Python CLI](/docs/software/python/cli/) — Command-line interface for advanced users
- [Android App](/docs/category/android-app) — Supports USB serial on Android

</TabItem>

**Network (WiFi)** is only available on **ESP32 devices**.

- [Web Client](https://client.meshtastic.org) — Connect by entering your device's IP address
- [Android App](/docs/category/android-app)
- [iOS / Apple App](/docs/category/apple-apps)
- [Python CLI](/docs/software/python/cli/)

</TabItem>

## Step 2: Set Your Region

Before your device can communicate over the mesh, you **must** set your LoRa region. This controls which radio frequency your device uses and must match your geographic location.

:::caution

Your device will not transmit until a region is set. Choose the correct region for where you are located — using the wrong region may violate local radio regulations.

:::

<TabItem value="android">

1. Install the [Meshtastic Android app](/docs/software/android/installation).
2. Open the app and connect to your device via Bluetooth or USB.
3. On the device card, tap **SET YOUR REGION**, or go to **Settings > LoRa**.
4. Select your region from the list and tap **Send**.

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="apple">

1. Install the Meshtastic app from the App Store.
2. Open the app and connect to your device via Bluetooth.
3. Go to **Settings > Radio Configuration > LoRa**.
4. Set your **Region** and save.

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="web">

1. Open [client.meshtastic.org](https://client.meshtastic.org) in Chrome or Edge.
2. Connect to your device (via Serial or Bluetooth).
3. Go to the **LoRa** settings page.
4. Under **Regional Settings**, select your **Region**.
5. Click **Save**.

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="cli">

1. Install the [Meshtastic Python CLI](/docs/software/python/cli/installation):
   ```sh
   pip3 install --upgrade meshtastic
   ```
2. Connect your device via USB and run the following command, replacing `US` with your region code from the table below:
   ```sh
   meshtastic --set lora.region US
   ```

</TabItem>

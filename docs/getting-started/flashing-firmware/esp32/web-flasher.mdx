The Web Flasher is the easiest way to install Meshtastic firmware on your ESP32 device. It runs in your browser — no software installation required.

## Flash Your Device

1. **Connect your device** to your computer with a USB data cable.
2. **Open the Web Flasher** at [flasher.meshtastic.org](https://flasher.meshtastic.org) using **Chrome** or **Edge** (other browsers are not supported).
3. **Follow the on-screen instructions** to select your device and flash the firmware.

That's it! The flasher will guide you through the rest.

:::tip Having trouble?

- Make sure you're using a **data cable**, not a charge-only cable.
- Try a different USB port.
- If the flasher doesn't detect your device, you may need to [install serial drivers](/docs/getting-started/serial-drivers/esp32).

:::

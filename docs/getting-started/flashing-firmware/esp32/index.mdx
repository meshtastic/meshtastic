## Choose a Flashing Method

There are three ways to flash firmware onto an ESP32 device. For most users, the **Web Flasher is the best choice** — it's fast, simple, and requires no software installation.

| Method | Best For | Difficulty |
|---|---|---|
| [**Web Flasher**](/docs/getting-started/flashing-firmware/esp32/web-flasher.mdx) (recommended) | Everyone, especially new users | Easy |
| [**CLI Script**](/docs/getting-started/flashing-firmware/esp32/cli-script) | Advanced users who prefer the command line | Intermediate |
| [**External Serial Adapter**](/docs/getting-started/flashing-firmware/esp32/external-serial-adapter) | Recovering devices that can't be flashed via USB | Advanced |

:::info Web Client Note

The [web client](/docs/software/web-client) at meshtastic.local is only updated when you do a full reinstall (not an update). To preserve your settings during a reinstall, [export your configuration](/docs/software/python/cli#--export-config) first and load it back after.

:::

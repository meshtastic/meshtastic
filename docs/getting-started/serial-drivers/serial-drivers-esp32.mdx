ESP32 devices connect to your computer through a USB-to-serial chip. You may need to install a driver for this chip before your computer can communicate with the device.

## Which Driver Do You Need?

Most ESP32 devices use one of two USB-to-serial chips:

- **CP210X** (Silicon Labs) — Common on older and many current devices
- **CH9102 / CH340 / CH341** (WCH) — Found on some newer boards

If you're not sure which chip your device uses, try installing the **CP210X** driver first. If your device still isn't detected, install the CH driver as well.

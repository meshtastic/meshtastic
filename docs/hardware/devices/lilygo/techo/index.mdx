## T-Echo

<DeviceImage device="t-echo.svg" alt="LILYGO T-Echo" size="lg" center />

The T-Echo by LILYGO® supports a low power consumption micro-controller and an e-ink display.

Further information on the LILYGO® T-Echo devices can be found on LILYGO®'s [GitHub page](https://github.com/Xinyuan-LilyGO/LilyGO-T-Echo).

### Specifications

- **MCU**
  - nRF52840 (Bluetooth BLE 5.0 & NFC)
- **LoRa Transceiver**
  - Semtech SX1262
- **Frequency options**
  - 433 MHz
  - 868 MHz
  - 915 MHz
- **Navigation Module**
  - L76K GNSS receiver (Supports GPS, BeiDou, GLONASS & QZSS)
- **Connectors**
  - U.FL antenna connector

### Features

- Reset, Program and capacitive touch buttons
- 1.54" eInk display
- Optional BME280 - Humidity and Pressure Sensor
- Comes with a case and battery

### Resources

- Firmware file: `firmware-t-echo-2.x.x.uf2`
- Purchase Links:

    - [Rokland 915MHz](https://msh.to/rokland-t-echo/)
    - [Rokland 915MHz w/ BME280 Kit](https://msh.to/rokland-t-echo-bme280/)
  - International
    - [LilyGO](https://msh.to/t-echo/)

## T-Echo Plus

<DeviceImage device="t-echo_plus.svg" alt="LILYGO T-Echo Plus" size="lg" center />

The T-Echo Plus is an enhanced version of the T-Echo, built on the nRF52840 with an E-Ink display, GNSS, a 6-axis sensor hub, and alerting hardware (buzzer and vibration motor).

### Specifications

- **MCU**
  - nRF52840 (Bluetooth BLE 5.0 & NFC)
- **LoRa Transceiver**
  - Semtech SX1262 (+22 dBm, -139 dBm sensitivity)
- **Frequency options**
  - 433 MHz
  - 868 MHz
  - 915 MHz
- **Navigation Module**
  - L76K GNSS receiver (Supports GPS, BeiDou, GLONASS & QZSS)
- **Sensors**
  - BHI260AP 6-axis IMU sensor hub
- **Connectors**
  - USB-C
  - U.FL antenna connector

### Features

- 1.54" E-Ink display
- Buzzer and vibration motor for alerts
- Li-Po battery with onboard charging
- Reset, Program and capacitive touch buttons
- Optional BME280 (temperature, humidity, pressure) variant available

### Resources

- Firmware file: `firmware-t-echo-plus-X.X.X.xxxxxxx.uf2`
- Purchase Links:
  - International
    - [LilyGO Wiki](https://wiki.lilygo.cc/products/t-echo-series/t-echo-plus/)

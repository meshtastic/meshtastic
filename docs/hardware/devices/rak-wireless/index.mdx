## RAK WisMesh Products
Besides the above mentioned modular hardware system "WisBlock", that allows to pick all components you want on your own, RAKwireless also offers a few meshtastic devices that come ready-to-go.

Those RAKwireless Meshtastic Devices (based on the above WisBlock components, and hence fully supported by Meshtatstic) are listed on their [Meshtastic Documentation Center page](https://docs.rakwireless.com/product-categories/meshtastic/).

This includes devices for different use cases like (not a complete list, just a few examples):
- the portable Meshtastic devices 
  - [WisMesh Pocket V2](https://docs.rakwireless.com/product-categories/meshtastic/wismesh-pocket-v2/overview/) and 
  - [WisMesh Pocket Mini](https://docs.rakwireless.com/product-categories/meshtastic/wismesh-pocket-mini/overview/) 
- the repeater devices 
  - [WisMesh Repeater](https://docs.rakwireless.com/product-categories/meshtastic/wismesh-repeater/overview/) and 
  - [WisMesh Repeater Ethernet](https://docs.rakwireless.com/product-categories/meshtastic/wismesh-eth-mqtt-gw/overview/) 
- as well as the [WisMesh Tab](https://docs.rakwireless.com/product-categories/meshtastic/wismesh-tap/overview/) device featuring a built-in on-screen keyboard allowing for stand-alone use.

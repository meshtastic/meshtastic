## ThinkNode M1

The ThinkNode M1 is a compact Meshtastic device featuring an nR52840 MCU, SX1262 LoRa transceiver, built-ing GPS, and a 1.54" e-ink display. It includes a 1200 mAh rechargeable battery, and comes pre-installed with Meshtastic with an optional durable enclosure.

### Specifications

- **MCU:**
  - NRF52840
    - Bluetooth 5.0
- **LoRa Transceiver:**
  - Semtech SX1262
- **Frequency Options:**
  - US (902.0Mhz to 928.0Mhz)
  - EU_868
- **Navigation Module:**
  - L76K
- **Antenna:**
  - RP-SMA
- **Connectors:**
  - USB-C

### Özellikler

- Meshtastic pre-installed.
- 1.54" e-ink display.
- 1200 mAh rechargeable battery.
- Integrated buzzer.
- Rotary encoder for backlight control.

## ThinkNode M2

The ThinkNode M2 is a compact Meshtastic device featuring an ESP32-S3 MCU, SX1262 LoRa transceiver, and a 1.3" OLED display. It includes a 1000 mAh rechargeable battery, and comes pre-installed with Meshtastic with an optional durable enclosure.

### Specifications

- **MCU:**
  - ESP32-S3
    - 2.4GHz Wi-Fi b/g/n
    - Bluetooth 4.2
- **LoRa Transceiver:**
  - Semtech SX1262
- **Frequency Options:**
  - US (902.0Mhz to 928.0Mhz)
  - EU_868
- **Antenna:**
  - U.FL/IPEX antenna connector for LoRa.
- **Connectors:**
  - USB-C

### Özellikler

- Meshtastic pre-installed.
- 1.3" OLED display.
- 1000 mAh rechargeable battery.
- Integrated buzzer.
